📝 Submit an Amendment

If the GPT tells you your suggestion requires an OS change — or you believe a parameter, threshold, or rule needs to change — submit it as an amendment on Slack.

Post in #system-design-reboot using this format:

[AMENDMENT]
What: Change quality evaluation window from 7 days to 10 days
Why: 7 days doesn't give new partners enough time to stabilize
Which OS: Quality OS (P01 parameter)

Amendments are acknowledged within 24 hours. Acceptance is not guaranteed. All amendments are reviewed by the System Design team (SD).

How We Work

  • System design drives workflow — not the other way around. The backend follows the OS logic, not ad-hoc corrections.
  • Standups = alignment only. No status recitation. If you're blocked, say it. If not, move on.
  • Small doubts = direct meeting with the relevant person. Don't wait for standup.
  • Design = agent-first. Build everything through AI agents. Manual intervention only when truly not possible.
  • Anyone can submit an amendment — but ask the GPT first to understand implications.
